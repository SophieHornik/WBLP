Biography

Jonathan (Jon) Hornik has lived in Marlboro his entire life. His father, Saul Hornik, served as Mayor of Marlboro for 12 years. Jon graduated Marlboro High School in 19XX. He attended the University of Delaware and graduated in 19XX with a major in XXXXX. He attended Brooklyn Law School and graduated in 19XX. Jon has been married to his wife Daryl Hornik since 1995. They have one son, Ethab (25), and two daughters, Olivia (23) and Sophia (18).

Jonathan Hornik has served as mayor of Marlboro Township, NJ for 18 years.

Mayor Hornik is the Chair of LHRG’s prestigious Private Lender Law Group—representing over 100+ nationwide lenders, originators, investors, including top Wall Street banks, Private Equity Firms, Family Offices and many others. Hornik advises public and private companies in all aspects of secured and unsecured loan and equity transactions.

Jon is also proud founding member and serves as general counsel for the National Private Lender’s Association (NPLA). He has also been the driving force in defeating proposed legislation that adversely impacts the industry.

In 2022, Hornik purchased the NPLA and NPLA Conference (formally the Pitbull Conference), the largest non-bank private lending conference in the country.
